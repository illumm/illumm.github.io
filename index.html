Why are we called illumm?

We want to shine a light on those who do not have access to resources that are beneficial to a student's future. When the pandemic became a major part of our lives, we realized that several other major components of life like education. The struggle that several families initially went through was now on a whole other level. The bridge between the privileged and underprivileged widened. We as illumm hope to narrow this gap by providing resources such as webinars, competitions, workshops, and other extracurricular resources to students. We want to offer skills by providing students all over the world with college and career advice and skills. We hope to build a community with people who are willing to hone new abilities for a brighter future.
