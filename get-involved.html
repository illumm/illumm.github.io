Join Our Team

Applications are open for our design, outreach/marketing, finance, local affairs, and tech/development teams. All high school and college students are eligible. Apply now .

Design

Works on the appearance of social media content. Discusses posts, captions, color scheme with the Director of Design. They will use the research gathered by the research committee and compile it posts in an eye-catching manner.

Outreach/Marketing

Works on researching new ways to expand the organization and carries out actions with the Director of Outreach/Marketing guidance. They are in charge of handling all of our social media platforms and ensuring our growth. Works on whatever assignment the director has given out.

Finance

Works on finding new ways and techniques regarding getting sponsorships, and funding for events, drives, and fundraisers with the Director of Finance. Works on whatever assignment the director has given out.

Local Affairs

Works on finding drives, events, and fundraisers with the Director of Research. Works on whatever assignment the director has given out. Conducts research on specific topics. The research will be used by the design committee.

Tech/Development

Work on developing the illumm website and make changes/tweaks as needed. Recommended to have working knowledge of Git (version management system), HTML, CSS, and JS, or willing to learn. Work to resolve discord/other technical issues that are seen by members of illumm.
